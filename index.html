"My wish, with all my heart, with all my energy, with all the passion I possess, is that in 50 years' time this book will not resemble a record of a lost world,"

Sebastião Salgado

Born in 1944, Salgado left a career in economics to start as a photographer in 1973.He worked on international assignments for a variety of photography agencies before forming his own, Amazonas Images, with his wife, Lélia, in 1994.



Over the years, Salgado's work has featured in numerous exhibitions and books, the latest of which brings together his Amazon photography.
